Legendary Ships

Queen Anne's Revenge

Queen Anne's Revenge

Queen Anne's Revenge was the legendary flagship commanded by the notorious pirate Blackbeard during the Golden Age of Piracy. Originally a French slave ship, it was captured and transformed into one of the most feared pirate vessels to sail the Caribbean Sea. Equipped with heavy cannons and a large crew, the ship intimidated merchant fleets and naval forces alike.

Known for its dark sails, powerful firepower, and terrifying reputation, the ship became a floating symbol of fear and freedom on the open ocean. Blackbeard used clever tactics, dramatic appearances, and ruthless strategy to dominate trade routes and capture valuable treasures.

Although the ship eventually ran aground off the coast of North Carolina in 1718, the legend of Queen Anne's Revenge continues to inspire stories, movies, and pirate folklore around the world. Today, it remains one of history’s most famous pirate ships ever discovered.
